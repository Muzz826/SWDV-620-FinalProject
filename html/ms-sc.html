How to Turn off Social Connector in Microsoft Outlook

I've talked to several people that find the Microsoft Office Social Connector to be an annoyance on the bottom of their screen as it tends up to take up needed space especially on smaller screens (mostly referring to laptops).

  1. Click on File --> Options
  2. On the Outlook Options screen click on Add-Ins
  3. Next you'll want to click on the Go... button located on bottom middle of the screen (see red arrow).


  4. On the last screen that opens you'll just need to take the checkmark out of the check box for the Microsoft Social Connector and then click OK.


  5. Once you've done this you'll see that the Social Connector is gone and you'll now have a little more reading room when going through emails.
    Note: The COM Add-ins screen is also a great place to either turn on or off other add-ins for Microsoft Office.
